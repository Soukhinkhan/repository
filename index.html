Welcomaae to Vinton, Virginia

The Vinton Area Chamber of Commerce provides information and support to the area's ever-growing business community. Our goal is to strengthen the social and economic environments of our community. We provide services for surrounding areas consisting of Roanoke, Salem, Bedford, Botetourt, and Smith Mountain Lake.

Explore this website to check out chamber programs, events, member benefits, the business directory, and consider joining this outstanding business organization. You'll also learn about our Board of Directors and our professional staff. We look forward to meeting you!

How Can We Help You?

Whether you’re a business seeking to increase exposure by taking advantage of our member benefits or simply looking
for area events hosted by Chamber members, we’re here to help you discover all that Vinton has to offer.
